Introducing D'Zoom

A brand new app that allows you to interrupt your virtual meeting by simulating a poor internet connection. The app works with the most popular video conferencing platforms and gives you control over when you want to be seen and heard, and when you want to make it look like your lapse in attention is entirely not your fault.

Simple and easy to use, just turn on D'Zoom and your video connection will give the impression of interruptions and technical difficulties, leaving the other participants none the wiser as to your true intentions.
